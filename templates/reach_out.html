About Us

We are a group of researchers that wish to make human feedback of generative models more accessible to the research community. We believe that the best way to do so is by creating platforms that allow users to easily and quickly (and for free) provide feedback through interaction with generative models. We hope that this specific effort will help to collect human feedback on text-to-image models, and will trigger the community to initiate similar efforts in other modalities. This is a community effort, and we would love to get your help and hear your feedback. If you have any suggestions, comments, or questions, please contact us at pickapick.io@gmail.com or through our discord channel .
